1.  Webinar spirit

OpenSIPS is one of the most advanced SIP engines available in the market. It is capable to handle large volumes of traffic and registered users. Come to this Webinar and learn about OpenSIPS from its main developer Bogdan Andrei Iancu.

2.  System Requirements

The webinars are hosted on gotomeeting.com platform. In order to be able to participate, you need :

  • Windows®-based attendees - Required: Windows® 2000, XP Home, XP Pro, 2003 Server, Vista
  • Macintosh®-based attendees - Required: Mac OS® X 10.4 (Tiger®) or newer

3.  Registration

Registration is free, but space is limited.
You need to register in order to reserve your Webinar seat - see the registration link from each webinar.

After registering you will receive a confirmation email containing information about joining the Webinar.
